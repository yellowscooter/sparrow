FriendsOfBooks™ Privacy Policy

 

FriendsOfBooks™ is strongly committed to protecting the privacy of our subscribers. The purpose of this privacy policy is to inform you what kinds of information we may gather about you when you visit www.friendsofbooks.com, how we may use that information, whether we disclose it to anyone, and the choices you have regarding our use of, and your ability to correct the information.
 

Collection and Use of Information About You

 

Personal information means information that can be used to identify and contact you, specifically your name, postal delivery address, e-mail address, payment method (e.g., credit card or debit card) and telephone number, as well as other information when such information is combined with your personal information.

 

Upon registration for membership, FriendsOfBooks™ will specifically ask for personal information about you. We use this information to provide you with FriendsOfBooks™ services. We use your email to communicate with you about your subscription. We use your telephone number only if there is subscription/delivery related issue. We may also use that information to let you know of additional products and services in which you might be interested. We may ask you for information about your interests and preferences to serve you better, but it’s your choice to respond or not.

 

Disclosure

Except as otherwise disclosed to you, we will not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties without notifying you of our intent to share the personal information in advance and giving you an opportunity to prevent your personal information from being shared.

We use other companies or agents perform services on our behalf. For example, we may provide postal addresses to our logistics partner for delivery purposes. We also partner with other companies to process credit card transactions. In the course of providing such services, these other companies may have access to your personal information. These companies are not permitted to use your personal information except for the purpose of providing the services we request of them.

From time to time, we may partner with companies whose products we believe will interest our users. In conjunction with these partners, we may send promotional announcements and/or serve web-based advertisements to our customers on their behalf.

FriendsOfBooks™ also reserves the right to disclose personal information when disclosure is required by law, if we reasonably believe disclosure is necessary to establish or exercise legal rights, or in situations involving potential threats to physical safety.

 

Security and Access

You can access your account from every page on the site by clicking the “My Account” tab, to update and correct your name, password, postal address, payment information and update your Bookshelf. We have implemented technology and security features, as well as strict internal guidelines, to safeguard the privacy of your personal information from unauthorized access or improper use. We will continue to enhance our security procedures as new technology becomes available.

 

No security system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure. Accordingly, we cannot guarantee the security of your personal information and cannot assume liability for improper access to it. By using this Web site, or providing personal information to us through any means, you agree that we can communicate with you electronically regarding security, privacy, and administrative issues relating to your use of this site.

We offer membership services only to consumers 18 years or older in age. While individuals under the age of 18 may utilize the service, they may do so only with the involvement of a parent or legal guardian and subject to our Terms of Use. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
