How FriendsOfBooks™ Service works

FriendsOfBooks™ is an online subscription based rental service that allows its subscribers to rent books through its website located at http://www.friendsofbooks.com.

To use FriendsofBooks™, subscriber will have to register with FriendsOfBooks.com™ to create an account. A subscriber can then browse/search books a subscriber would like to read, and add them to his/her Bookshelf. Once payment has been received or we have made an arrangement with the subscriber to pickup the payment at the time of delivery, the first set of books will be delivered to the subscriber at the shipping address provided at the time of registration. FriendsOfBooks™ rents out two (2) books at a time. Once subscriber is done with the first set of books, subscriber goes to www.friendsofbooks.com and navigates to "My Bookshelf" tab and click on "Ready for Pickup" button. FriendsOfBooks™ will pickup the previous set of books from the subscribers shipping address, and deliver the next set of books, within two business days.
